Get Started

Your First Trade in Four Steps

Opening an HNB Stockbrokers account typically completes within two business days. Once your CDS account is activated, you can place your first order immediately.

1

Open a CDS Account

All CSE trades require a Central Depository System account. HNB Stockbrokers processes your CDS application — you do not need to contact the CSE directly.

2

Complete KYC & Account Opening

Submit NIC or passport, proof of address, and a bank statement. Complete the SEC-mandated investor profile questionnaire. Takes under 20 minutes online.

3

Fund Your Trading Account

Transfer funds via IBFT to your HNB Stockbrokers trust account. Minimum LKR 10,000 for DIY accounts. Funds reflect within 30 minutes during business hours.

4

Place Your First Order

Log into HNB DIY, select your stock, set price and quantity, and confirm. Your dealer will call you when your first order is matched if you are on the advisory tier.
