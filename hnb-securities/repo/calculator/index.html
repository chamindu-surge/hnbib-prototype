Methodology

How Your Return Is Calculated

Step 1: Gross Interest
Interest = Principal × Rate × Days / 365 Example: LKR 1,000,000 × 9.20% × 91/365 = LKR 22,959
Step 2: WHT Deduction
WHT = Gross Interest × WHT Rate At 5%: LKR 22,959 × 5% = LKR 1,148 deducted
Step 3: Net Maturity Value
Maturity = Principal + Net Interest LKR 1,000,000 + (22,959 − 1,148) = LKR 1,021,811
Important notes: WHT is deducted at source by HNB Securities and remitted to the IRD. For resident individuals, WHT is the final tax — no further income tax declaration required on Repo income. Corporates are exempt from WHT but must include Repo income in taxable profits. Non-residents are subject to 14% WHT unless a DTA applies.
Methodology

Calculation Basis & Frequently Asked Questions

The Formula

// Step 1: Gross interest
grossInt = principal × (rate/100) × (days/365)

// Step 2: WHT (if applicable)
wht = grossInt × (whtRate/100)

// Step 3: Net maturity value
maturityValue = principal + grossInt − wht
Day count convention: Actual/365. CBSL uses the actual number of days in the Repo period divided by 365, regardless of whether the year is a leap year.

Common Questions

Is my return guaranteed?
Yes — the rate is contractually fixed at placement. HNB Securities' obligation is backed by government securities held in your name in LankaSecure.
Can I roll over on maturity?
Yes. HNB Securities will contact you before maturity with the prevailing rate for your next tenor. You can roll over principal only or principal plus interest.
What if I need funds before maturity?
Early termination is possible subject to a breakage fee (typically 0.5–1% of interest accrued). Overnight and call repos may be terminated with same-day notice before 11:00 AM.
