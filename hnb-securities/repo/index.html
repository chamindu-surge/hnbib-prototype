What Is a Repo

Government-Backed Returns, Daily Liquidity

A Repurchase Agreement (Repo) is a short-term secured borrowing arrangement. When you invest in a Repo through HNB Securities, you purchase government securities from us under an agreement to sell them back at a fixed price on a specified date. The difference between the purchase and repurchase price is your return — fully known at the time you invest.

Because the underlying collateral is Government of Sri Lanka Treasury Bills or Treasury Bonds — guaranteed instruments — Repos are considered the lowest credit-risk domestic investment available in the Sri Lankan market.

1

You Invest

Deposit minimum LKR 100,000. Choose tenor from overnight to 364 days. Rate is locked at entry.

2

Securities Held as Collateral

HNB Securities registers the government securities in your name in the LankaSecure depository system (operated by CBSL).

3

On Maturity

Your principal plus the agreed interest is credited to your account on the maturity date. Same-day settlement via RTGS.

4

Roll or Redeem

At maturity you may roll into a new Repo at prevailing rates or redeem in full. No penalty for redemption on maturity date.

How to Invest

Open a Repo Account in Three Steps

Existing HNB Group customers can activate Repo investment within one business day. New customers complete full onboarding in 48–72 hours.

1. Open Your Account

Complete the HNB Securities client application — NIC, proof of address, and source of funds declaration. Existing HNB Bank current account holders require only a one-page supplementary form.

2. Fund Your Account

Transfer your investment amount (minimum LKR 100,000) to your HNB Securities settlement account via CEFTS or RTGS. Same-day credits accepted until 2:00 PM on business days.

3. Place Your Repo

Call, email, or use the HNB Securities client portal to specify your tenor and amount. Your rate is confirmed immediately. Securities are registered in LankaSecure the same day.

Documents Required
National ID / Passport · Bank statement (3 months) · Source of funds declaration · Tax Identification Number (TIN)

Eligible Investors

Sri Lankan individuals (resident and non-resident), corporates, trusts, and institutions. Joint accounts available. No minimum age restriction for adults.

Tax Treatment

Interest income from Repos is subject to 5% Withholding Tax (WHT) for resident individuals. Rates quoted are gross; HNB Securities will deduct and remit WHT on your behalf.

Early Redemption

Early redemption is available on request, subject to a breakage fee. Overnight and call repos may be terminated with same-day notice before 11:00 AM.
