What Is a T-Bill

Short-Term, Government-Guaranteed Returns

Treasury Bills (T-Bills) are short-term debt instruments issued by the Government of Sri Lanka through the Central Bank (CBSL). They are sold at a discount to face value and redeemed at face value on maturity — the difference being your return.

T-Bills are issued in three tenors: 91 days (3 months), 182 days (6 months), and 364 days (12 months). Because the GoSL guarantees full repayment, T-Bills carry the highest domestic credit rating and set the benchmark risk-free rate for all LKR financial instruments.

HNB Securities, as a CBSL Primary Dealer, participates directly in weekly CBSL T-Bill auctions — giving clients access to primary market pricing and same-day execution on secondary market T-Bills.

No Coupon — Pure Discount

T-Bills pay no periodic interest. The entire return is the difference between purchase price and face value — simple, transparent, and tax-efficient.

Weekly Auctions

CBSL auctions T-Bills every Wednesday. HNB Securities participates as a Primary Dealer, passing competitive allocation to clients the same day.

WHT at 5%

The discount (return) is subject to 5% Withholding Tax. No additional income tax declaration needed for individuals — WHT is the final tax.
