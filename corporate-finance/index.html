What We Do

Deep capital market expertise, proven in every cycle

HNB Investment Bank's Corporate Finance practice has been at the centre of Sri Lanka's capital market activity since 1992. We have structured some of the most complex and high-profile transactions in the country's history — from the Union Bank IPO that drew 300 times subscription, to the NTT sale that established a new benchmark for cross-border M&A advisory.

Our team brings together capital markets structuring, regulatory expertise, and a distribution network built across decades of primary market activity. We advise both buyers and sellers, and we execute.

How We Work

Advisory that executes

01
Mandate and structure

We begin with your capital objectives and market conditions. Our team proposes the optimal structure, instrument type, and timing before a single document is drafted.

02
Regulatory and preparation

We manage the regulatory process end-to-end — SEC filings, prospectus preparation, and CSE liaison. Your team focuses on the business while we handle the compliance pathway.

03
Distribution and close

HNB's distribution network — institutional, HNW, and retail — is activated for the placement. We close the transaction and ensure full subscription.

How We Work

From mandate to close

1
Engage

Initial mandate discussion, NDA execution, and objectives mapping. Typically 1–2 weeks.

2
Structure

Instrument selection, pricing analysis, and regulatory pathway design. Typically 2–4 weeks.

3
