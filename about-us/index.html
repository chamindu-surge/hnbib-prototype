Our Heritage

Thirty years of capital markets, one trusted name

HNB Investment Bank is the capital markets subsidiary of Hatton National Bank PLC — Sri Lanka's largest listed private bank. Our roots extend to 1992, when Lanka Ventures was established as one of the country's first licensed venture capital companies.

Through three decades of market cycles, privatisations, and landmark transactions, we built a depth of experience that few institutions in this market can match. Our teams have structured IPOs, advised on mergers, arranged LKR billion-scale debt issuances, and managed fixed-income portfolios through every episode of market stress.

Today HNB Investment Bank consolidates four distinct businesses — Stockbrokers, Securities, Corporate Finance, and Lanka Ventures — under one capital-market brand, backed by the balance sheet strength and regulatory standing of HNB PLC.
