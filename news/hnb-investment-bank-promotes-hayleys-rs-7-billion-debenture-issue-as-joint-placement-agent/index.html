HNB Investment Bank Acts as Joint Placement Agent for Hayleys LKR 7 Billion Debenture Issue

HNB Investment Bank has been appointed as Joint Placement Agent for Hayleys PLC's LKR 7 billion listed debenture issue — the largest corporate debenture issue on the Colombo Stock Exchange in 2026 and one of the largest in Sri Lanka's capital market history.

By HNB IB Corporate Finance

Deal Announcement 22 May 2026 By HNB IB Corporate Finance

The Hayleys debenture issue, approved by the Securities and Exchange Commission in May 2026, will be issued in two tranches: a LKR 4 billion fixed-rate Tranche A at 13.00% p.a. and a LKR 3 billion fixed-rate Tranche B at 13.50% p.a. Both tranches carry a five-year tenure and will be listed on the CSE Debt Securities Board.

HNB Investment Bank's role as Joint Placement Agent involves co-managing the institutional subscription process alongside the other placement agents. HNB IB's fixed income distribution network — spanning licensed banks, insurance companies, unit trusts, and high-net-worth individual investors — contributed to the book being substantially covered within the first week of the roadshow.

Hayleys Group Chairman Mohan Pandithage commented: "HNB Investment Bank brings both distribution depth and market credibility to this transaction. Their placement capability across institutional and retail investor segments is a critical element of our capital raising strategy."

For HNB IB, the Hayleys debenture represents the capstone of a strong FY2026 debt capital markets pipeline that includes the Fintrex high-yield bond, the Singer Finance SDTC, and several pending corporate debt mandates. The firm's Debt Capital Markets team has been a consistent top-three arranger on the CSE's Debt Securities Board over the past three years.

"HNB Investment Bank brings both distribution depth and market credibility to this transaction."
Mohan Pandithage, Chairman — Hayleys PLC
