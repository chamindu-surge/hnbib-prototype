HNB Investment Bank Facilitates Sri Lanka's First-Ever High-Yield Bond Issuance for Fintrex Finance

HNB Investment Bank has acted as Sole Lead Manager and Arranger for Sri Lanka's first formally structured high-yield bond issuance, successfully placing a LKR 1.5 billion senior secured note for Fintrex Finance Limited — a landmark transaction that establishes a new asset class in the domestic debt capital market.

By HNB IB Corporate Finance

Deal Announcement 19 February 2026 By HNB IB Corporate Finance

The transaction, rated [B+] by ICRA Lanka, carries a fixed coupon of 16.75% per annum with a three-year bullet maturity. The notes are secured against a first-ranking charge over Fintrex's vehicle leasing receivables portfolio, valued at LKR 2.1 billion at the time of issuance — providing 1.4x collateral coverage.

High-yield bonds — instruments rated below investment grade but offering commensurately higher coupons — are a cornerstone of developed debt capital markets but have historically been absent from Sri Lanka's corporate debt landscape. HNB IB's Corporate Finance team spent 14 months developing the legal and structural framework in consultation with the Securities and Exchange Commission and legal advisors from D.L. & F. De Saram.

Fintrex Finance CEO Nilusha Weerakoon stated: "Accessing debt capital at market-rate pricing was transformative for our business. HNB Investment Bank not only structured this transaction but created the infrastructure for an entirely new financing instrument in Sri Lanka."

HNB IB CEO Ray Abeywardena described the transaction as a milestone: "This is not just a deal — it is proof that Sri Lanka's capital markets can accommodate the full complexity of modern corporate finance. We expect high-yield instruments to become a meaningful part of the debt capital market over the next five years."
