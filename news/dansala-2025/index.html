HNB Investment Bank Operates Vesak Dansala in Colombo 10

HNB Investment Bank operated a traditional Vesak Dansala at its HNB Tower premises on T.B. Jayah Mawatha, Colombo 10, serving over 3,500 members of the public during the Vesak Full Moon Poya Day celebrations.

By HNB IB Communications

CSR 23 May 2026 By HNB IB Communications

The Dansala, a free food offering observed during Vesak as an act of generosity and merit, was organised by HNB IB staff with support from the HNB Group's Corporate Social Responsibility committee. Traditional Sri Lankan meals including rice, dhal curry, and sweetmeats were served from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Over 120 HNB IB employees volunteered across cooking, serving, and logistics duties. The Dansala has been an annual HNB Group tradition since 2009, and HNB Investment Bank has hosted its own dedicated location since 2018.

Managing Director Damith Pallewatte participated in the morning session: "Vesak is a time for reflection and giving. This Dansala is a small expression of our gratitude to the community that has supported HNB IB's growth."

The 2025 Dansala was notable for incorporating eco-friendly packaging across all food items, in line with HNB Group's Sustainability Charter commitments to reduce single-use plastics at all corporate events.

"Vesak is a time for reflection and giving. This Dansala is a small expression of our gratitude."
Damith Pallewatte, MD/COO — HNB Investment Bank
