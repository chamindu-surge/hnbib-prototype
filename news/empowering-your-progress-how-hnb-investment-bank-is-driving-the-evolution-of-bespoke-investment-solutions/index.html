Empowering Your Progress: How HNB Investment Bank Is Driving the Evolution of Bespoke Investment Solutions

Sri Lanka's capital markets are entering a new phase — one defined by institutional deepening, retail participation, and cross-border investment flows. At HNB Investment Bank, we believe that progress is not a destination but a discipline, and that the role of an investment bank is to translate complex market conditions into actionable opportunity for every client.

By Ray Abeywardena, CEO

Thought Leadership 8 April 2026 By Ray Abeywardena, CEO

Three structural forces are reshaping Sri Lanka's investment landscape. First, post-restructuring sovereign debt recovery is creating a new fixed income yield environment — one that rewards duration positioning and active management over passive holding. Second, the CSE's equity market is undergoing a quality re-rating, with institutional foreign flows returning to select large-cap counters for the first time since 2022. Third, domestic retail investors — empowered by mobile trading platforms and lower brokerage costs — are entering the market in unprecedented numbers.

HNB Investment Bank's integrated platform is purpose-built to serve all three dimensions. HNB Securities, our CBSL-licensed Primary Dealer, provides direct access to government securities at competitive rates with same-day settlement. HNB Stockbrokers, our CSE member firm, combines 48-stock research coverage with a modern online trading platform. And our Corporate Finance advisory desk has led over LKR 28 billion in capital market transactions in FY2025.

The word 'bespoke' is often misused in financial services. At HNB IB, bespoke means something specific: a solution designed around your duration, yield target, liquidity requirement, and risk tolerance — not a packaged product with your name attached. Our relationship managers are trained to ask different questions, not sell predetermined answers.

Looking ahead, we see four themes driving FY2026: the normalisation of interest rates as CBSL continues its easing cycle, the resurgence of IPO activity on the CSE, the maturation of Sri Lanka's corporate debt market, and the integration of ESG considerations into institutional portfolio construction. HNB IB is positioned at the intersection of all four.

"Bespoke means a solution designed around your requirements — not a packaged product with your name attached."
Ray Abeywardena, CEO — HNB Investment Bank
