HNB Investment Bank Powers Alpha Fire Services' Journey: From Historic CSE Listing to Sale of Controlling Interest

HNB Investment Bank has completed a landmark dual-mandate advisory engagement for Alpha Fire Services PLC — first acting as Listing Advisor for the company's initial public offering on the Colombo Stock Exchange, and subsequently as M&A Advisor for the sale of a 62% controlling interest to a regional fire safety conglomerate.

By HNB IB Corporate Finance

Deal Announcement 7 April 2026 By HNB IB Corporate Finance

Alpha Fire Services PLC, Sri Lanka's largest integrated fire safety services provider, listed on the CSE's Main Board in November 2025 at an IPO price of LKR 24.00 per share, raising LKR 480 million in fresh equity. HNB Investment Bank served as Listing Advisor and Joint Placement Agent alongside HNB Stockbrokers, which managed the retail allotment process.

Five months after listing, HNB IB Corporate Finance was engaged to advise Alpha Fire's founding shareholders on the sale of a 62% strategic stake to Siemens Fire Safety Pte Ltd (Singapore), a subsidiary of the Siemens Smart Infrastructure division. The transaction valued Alpha Fire at LKR 3.2 billion — a 40% premium to the post-IPO trading average.

Alpha Fire Services Founder and outgoing Chairman Suresh Perera commented: "When we first approached HNB Investment Bank for our IPO, we had a vision for what Alpha Fire could become. The M&A outcome has exceeded even our most optimistic projections. HNB IB understood our business and brought exactly the right strategic buyer to the table."

The transaction structure allowed founding shareholders to realise partial liquidity while retaining a meaningful 38% stake. Siemens Fire Safety Pte Ltd will bring global technology integration, regional distribution, and access to Siemens' institutional client base in Sri Lanka and the Maldives.

"HNB IB understood our business and brought exactly the right strategic buyer to the table."
Suresh Perera, Founder & Chairman — Alpha Fire Services PLC
